Nestled in rural Co. Down, the workshop is managed by experienced cabinet maker Andrew Morgan

Specialising in the manufacture of furniture and bespoke joinery items for both domestic and contract markets by combining traditional techniques with modern machinery to fulfil the design brief of pleasing, functional and practical items at an affordable price. With a varied experience of different woodworking processes and techniques, traditional workmanship is greatly appreciated against the mass produced and sub-standard items of furniture and joinery. You are welcome to visit the workshop by appointment only or alternatively, I could visit you on site to discuss projects.

Domestic furniture

Planning, making and installation work catered for every room no matter how big or small we’ve been in them all from fully equipped kitchens, media units, home studies, bedrooms, walk in wardrobe rooms to the WC.

Bespoke joinery

This service is mainly specialising in unique statement entrance doorways for building or joinery contractors whether it’s for a new build or to fit within an existing opening we are flexible to achieve the best outcome our contractors and clients.

Wood machining

Primarily for joinery contractors we have the means to reproduce or match mouldings to a provided sample. This machining service also offers site contractors without a premises a means for prepared stock not readily available off the shelf.
